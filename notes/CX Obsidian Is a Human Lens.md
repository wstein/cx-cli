Obsidian is a human navigation lens for the note graph, not the authority for repository semantics.

## What

Humans may use Obsidian to browse backlinks, maps of content, and note neighborhoods.

## Why

Repository truth must remain portable and tool-independent. If semantics live in editor plugins or vault-specific conventions, the system becomes fragile.

## How

`cx` owns validation, extraction, graph semantics, and doc compilation. Obsidian exports may be generated from `cx`, but not the other way around.
